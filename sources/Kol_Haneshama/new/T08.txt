/PR

In reciting the Kaddish we affirm our awareness of holiness in
our world. Much of our experience of divine goodness, grace
and love has come to us through those whose lives have touched
our own. (Today we remember...) We invoke the transcendent
power of love and caring as we sanctify Godòs name./PR

Love is not changed by Death, and nothing is lost and all in the end is
harvest./PR

Edith Sitwell/PR
